Fendi would like to do more MTO CUSTOM events per year. For that reason, they expressed the need to have a customization tool able to assist them in the customization of a full customisable bag.

  • Scuola Politecnica of Design
  • UX-UI
  • Digital Strategy

Context

Although in the Luxury market there are configurators that allow users to carry out guided personalizations, Fendi's case is more complex, due the nature of the service bag’s and the production and the data that is necessary to capture and transfer to the production facility.

Challenge

Develop a UX strategy that will show how FENDI could improve MTO Custom Service not just focusing on basic customisation rules.

Solution

Feni by you: a smart assistant able to assist MTO Artisans in all the steps of the service making easier to understand what customer want and being able to give it to them.

UX/UI

The solution is based on a conversational UI in order to train sales Associates and a real-time 3D configurator.
