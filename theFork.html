Challenge

According to our insights by doing research like: guerrilla testing, online survey and interviews, our challenge was let user find and book the best restaurant with the best discount making the user’s experience easy and pleasurable.

Process

We improved step by step our solution by doing user testing. As a result We added new features, improved existing ones and given a new look. It's based on two colors in order to distinguish UI elements.

Design

People can easily find restaurants related to their need thanks to a better IA and a usable search box.
